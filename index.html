Who we are

A steady hand for the places people call home.

We're a family-owned property management company based in Annapolis, Maryland. For more than forty years, FMC and its affiliated companies have managed apartment communities across the Mid-Atlantic — more than 550 homes, most of them affordable housing in the towns and counties where our teams live and work.

Our work is quiet and consistent: well-kept grounds, maintenance that shows up, fair policies, and offices that answer the phone. It's why our communities stay near 95% occupancy, and why many keep waitlists.
