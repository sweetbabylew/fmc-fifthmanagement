Our Communities

Sixteen communities across Maryland and Virginia.

Apartments and townhomes in seven counties — most of them affordable communities, including three for seniors, plus one market-rate community. Each has its own site with floor plans, photos, office hours, and how to apply.

Looking to rent? Each community handles its own leasing — visit its site below or call its office directly. Current residents can use the resident portal to pay rent or request maintenance.
