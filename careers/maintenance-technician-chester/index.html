Maintenance

Maintenance Technician

Keep two Kent Island communities running — the day-to-day maintenance of the property, from plumbing and electrical to carpentry and turnovers.

Apply on Indeed Ask a question

About the role

What you'll do

  • Handle the day-to-day maintenance of the property.
  • Plumbing, electrical, drywall, and painting work.
  • Install LVT flooring.
  • Carpentry — installing exterior and interior doors and kitchen cabinets.
  • Help the property stay compliant with USDA Rural Development regulations, ADA requirements, and company policy.

What we're looking for

  • Hands-on experience across plumbing, electrical, drywall, painting, flooring, and carpentry.
  • A valid driver's license and reliable transportation.
  • Working knowledge of Fair Housing regulations.
  • Exceptional people skills — you'll work with residents and vendors in person, and clear verbal and written communication is required.
  • Prior property-maintenance experience is a plus.

What we offer

  • $52,000 a year, full-time.
  • Health, dental, and life insurance.
  • 401(k) plan.
  • Paid time off.
