About Fifth Management

A family company that has quietly done the work for more than twenty years.

Fifth Management & Consulting is a family-owned, women-led property management company based in Annapolis, Maryland — grown one community and one resident relationship at a time.

Our mission

Make residents proud of where they live.

We believe property management is a service profession. Every interaction — a maintenance call, a recertification, a lease renewal — is a chance to do right by the people who depend on us.

That belief shapes how we hire, how we train, and how we measure success: more than 550 homes across sixteen communities, roughly 95% occupancy, and waitlists at many of our properties.

Our values

What guides our work.

Care

Our communities are homes first. We keep them places people are proud to live.

Consistency

Long-tenured teams, predictable processes, and steady communication with residents.

Integrity

Fair, transparent policies for residents and honest compliance with the programs our communities operate under.

Local roots

Our teams live in the counties they serve — Southern Maryland, the Eastern Shore, northern Maryland, and Virginia's Northern Neck are home.

What we do

Full-service management, in-house.

We handle the whole job ourselves: leasing, financials, resident care, unit preparation, maintenance and repairs, and construction management. Our maintenance staff is on call at every community — residents never wait on a third-party contractor to get a door fixed.

Most of our portfolio is affordable apartment communities under the USDA Rural Development Section 515 program — including three senior communities — alongside a market-rate community and a mix of single-family, mixed-use, and office properties. Compliance, certifications, and waitlists are daily work for us, not an afterthought.

Residents do the everyday things online: pay rent, request maintenance, and message the office through the resident portal.

Leadership

The people behind Fifth Management.

KW

Kate Weaver

President

Kate has been with Fifth Management and its affiliated companies for more than thirty years and leads the overall management of the business. She helped establish the systems the company runs on today and has directed the development of its property management services throughout. JD

Jennine has been with Fifth Management and affiliates since 2004 and manages human resources, accounts payable, and accounts receivable. She administers the company's benefit plans, payroll, and employee programs, and oversees banking and cash management for the corporate office.

KJ

Katie Jenkins

Compliance Officer

Katie has been with Fifth Management since 2003 and is responsible for compliance with the government regulations our communities operate under — reviewing tenant certifications and monitoring waitlists, tenant files, and electronic records. She also helps train new employees, runs in-house management workshops, and assists with tenant appeals.
