About Fifth Management

A family company that has quietly done the work for more than forty years.

Fifth Management & Consulting is a family-owned property management company based in Annapolis, Maryland — grown one community and one resident relationship at a time.

Our mission

Make residents proud of where they live.

We believe property management is a service profession. Every interaction — a maintenance call, a recertification, a lease renewal — is a chance to do right by the people who depend on us.

That belief shapes how we hire, how we train, and how we measure success: more than 550 homes across sixteen communities, roughly 95% occupancy, and waitlists at many of our properties.

Our values

What guides our work.

Care

Our communities are homes first. We keep them places people are proud to live.

Consistency

Long-tenured teams, predictable processes, and steady communication with residents.

Integrity

Fair, transparent policies for residents and honest compliance with the programs our communities operate under.

Local roots

Our corporate office is in Annapolis, and our teams live in the counties they serve — Southern Maryland, the Eastern Shore, northern Maryland, and Virginia's Northern Neck are home.

What we do

Full-service management, in-house.

We handle the whole job ourselves: leasing, financials, resident care, unit preparation, maintenance and repairs, and construction management. Our maintenance staff is on call at every community — residents never wait on a third-party contractor to get a door fixed.

Most of our portfolio is affordable apartment communities under the USDA Rural Development Section 515 program — including three senior communities — alongside a market-rate community and a mix of single-family, mixed-use, and office properties. Compliance, certifications, and waitlists are daily work for us, not an afterthought. Every FMC employee is certified in fair housing, with additional training required every year.

Residents do the everyday things online: pay rent, request maintenance, and message the office through the resident portal.

Kate Weaver

President & Treasurer

Kate Weaver has been part of FMC's family of companies for more than four decades. She joined in 1984, rose through senior financial leadership as Comptroller, then CFO, and has served as President since 2012. Today she provides the company's fiscal, strategic, and operational leadership: setting policy, directing long-range planning, and leading the accounting, human resources, and financial teams that support dozens of staff members across the organization.

Kate's financial expertise guides every part of the business. Over her career, she has directed cash management, financing arrangements, loan servicing, auditing, financial reporting and more. Our organizations, overseen by Kate, have been involved in acquisition, development, construction, syndication, and affordable housing administration. Kate is an officer in each of our companies.

Kate earned her Maryland CPA license and holds a B.S. in Business Administration and Accounting and an M.B.A., both from the University of Maryland University College.

Dexter Clark

Vice President of Property Management & Operations

Dexter Clark serves as Vice President of Property Management & Operations for Fifth Management & Consulting, Inc., bringing more than 20 years of leadership experience in multifamily affordable housing, market-rate communities, and commercial real estate assets.

He provides strategic direction across property operations: regulatory compliance, occupancy growth, maintenance, budgeting, capital improvements, and team development. Dexter is recognized for building high-performing teams and fostering a culture centered on accountability, communication, collaboration, and exceptional customer service.

Dexter holds a Property Manager License from the District of Columbia Department of Licensing and Consumer Protection (DLCP) and has earned the Certified Occupancy Specialist (COS) and Tax Credit Specialist (TCS) certifications through the National Center for Housing Management (NCHM), demonstrating his expertise in affordable housing compliance and property management.

He serves on the Board of Directors for the IREM Maryland Chapter and is an active committee member with the Council for Affordable and Rural Housing (CARH), the Maryland Affordable Housing Coalition (MAHC), and the Maryland Multi-Housing Association (MMHA). Through these professional affiliations, he collaborates with industry leaders to advance best practices, strengthen housing communities, and support the continued growth of the property management profession.

Dexter is passionate about mentoring the next generation of industry leaders, advancing professional excellence, and building thriving communities for residents, clients, ownership partners, and the teams he leads.

Katie Jenkins

Compliance & Systems Director

Katie Jenkins has been with Fifth Management & Consulting, Inc. since 2003, now in her third decade of dedicated service. As Compliance & Systems Director, she combines hands-on operational leadership with deep regulatory expertise to drive exceptional living experiences and operational excellence across FMC's communities.

Katie ensures FMC's communities meet the government regulations under which they operate, overseeing tenant certifications, monitoring waitlists, and maintaining the accuracy of tenant files and electronic records. She also trains new employees, facilitates in-house management workshops, and supports the tenant appeals process, making sure every team member has the knowledge and tools to uphold the highest compliance standards.

Katie specializes in building positive manager-resident relationships, developing strong, well-trained teams, and keeping properties running efficiently and in full compliance. Known for her collaborative style and commitment to a job done right, she thrives on solving complex challenges, streamlining operations, and fostering welcoming communities where residents feel at home.

Katie takes pride in coaching team members and delivering results that benefit residents and property owners alike, a people-first approach reflected in more than two decades of steady, trusted leadership at Fifth Management. If you catch her outside of work, she'll have her hands full with arts & crafts, accompanied by her big dog.
